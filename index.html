FAQ

Things people ask before they ignite

Does Nova train on our data?

No. Workspace content is never used to train shared models. You can also set an automatic deletion window per workspace.

Can Nova match our existing document formats?

Yes — upload a few examples and Nova mirrors structure, tone, and section order going forward.

What happens when I hit my plan's limit?

You'll get a warning at 80% usage. Nothing is cut off mid-task — you can finish what you started, then upgrade or wait for the next cycle.

Is there an API?

Yes, available on Team and Enterprise plans, with the same transform engine that powers the app.
